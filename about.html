About Us

The Company

A Member of Vendor Junction Group

Established in 2016, Vendor Junction is a knowledge-based professional service and management firm specializing in international business development, channel sales ecosystems, and in-country business operations management for our clients in Europe & North America.

Our industry-specific managers, business unit teams, and alliance partners identify high-potential markets with data-driven go-to-market strategies. With defined business processes that align with our client's vision, Vendor Junction delivers margin-based new revenue lines across the Middle East, Africa & South Asia.

We create defined deliverables for every step of executing the set strategy, ensuring a unified customer experience, sustained revenue growth, and measurable return on investment across high-growth and emerging international markets.

Headquartered in the United Arab Emirates and with representative offices in the USA, Finland, Sweden, Denmark, Saudi Arabia, India, and Pakistan, Vendor Junction operates internationally with global reach for its clients.

CEO and Founder

Sayed Jawed is a business development, channel sales, marketing and management professional who has earlier worked with Cisco Systems, Tech Data Corporation, Westcon Group and Extreme Networks in leadership positions in the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia markets.

He is an industry-recognized individual for driving accelerated growth across the region across industries in corporate accounts, value-added distribution, and sales channels with critical strengths in leveraging local market dynamics, devising routes to market entry, brand positioning, channel partners management and localised marketing for enterprise, SMB, and consumer business sectors.

A seasoned business leader, negotiator, and public speaker, Sayed drives new businesses with product & market-specific strategic planning, financial analysis, risk management, marketing, quality systems implementation and operations infrastructure.

Sayed is a Board Member of iSTOC Oy and holds the following positions:

  • Advisory board member, NE Device SW
  • Advisory board member, Kipuwex Ltd Business partner, Kajaani
  • University of Applied Sciences International Alliances
  • Collaboration partner, Allied ICT Finland
